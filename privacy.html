Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

At Cliply, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable privacy laws.

1. Who We Are

Cliply is a video publishing and analytics platform. For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@cliply.io.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you use Cliply:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password (hashed)
  • TikTok data: access tokens, account identifiers, and analytics data retrieved via the TikTok API on your behalf
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, timestamps, and device/browser information
  • Content data: videos and metadata you upload for scheduling and publishing
  • Communication data: emails or messages you send to our support team

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Cliply service:

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To publish videos to TikTok on your behalf as instructed
  • To retrieve and display analytics from your TikTok account
  • To send you service-related notifications and updates
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To monitor platform performance and detect misuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

4. TikTok API Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we access data through TikTok's official API. This data is used exclusively to provide the services you request (publishing, analytics). We store TikTok access tokens securely and revoke them upon account disconnection. Our use of TikTok API data complies with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Platform Policies.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted third-party services (hosting, email) that process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal obligations: when required by law or valid legal process
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger or acquisition, with prior notice to you

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, and for up to 12 months after account deletion for legal and audit purposes. Video files uploaded for publishing are deleted within 30 days of successful publication or upon your request.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cliply.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted storage of tokens and passwords, and regular security audits. No method of transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to promptly notifying you of any breach affecting your data.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email. Continued use of Cliply after the changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@cliply.io.
